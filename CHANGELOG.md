- Rewrote Phase B cluster joining from scratch: replaced the broken bridge-face-alignment approach (which computed positions for rotations it never applied, causing overlaps and 1-face connections) with a straightforward place-check-move-rotate strategy — for each cluster, probe candidate positions near existing tiles, try all 6 hex rotations of the whole cluster as a rigid body (rotating both relative offsets and individual tile rotations), check every tile for polygon overlap against every placed tile, score by total cross-cluster contact points, require `MIN_CONTACT_POINTS` (4 = 2 faces), and pick the best valid placement. Falls back to hex-ring scan at increasing depth if face-adjacent probes fail. Clusters that can't be placed without overlap are dropped entirely.
- Fixed cluster offset coordinates causing off-grid placement and whole-dungeon overlaps: `relX/relY` were stored relative to the cluster centroid (an averaged non-hex-grid point), so adding them to a snapped base put every tile off-grid. Changed to store offsets relative to `kept[0]` (the anchor tile), whose relX/relY are 0,0. Now the anchor snaps to the hex grid and all other tiles maintain their hex-aligned offsets. Also fixed `snapTileCenterToHex` being called without the correct rotation for the cluster angle being tested (entrance tiles have rotation-dependent snap anchors).
